Additionally, long flights can increase the risk of blood clots, so continuing your blood thinner as prescribed is often important. Your doctor might also recommend other preventive measures during flights, such as:

- Moving around or stretching your legs regularly
- Wearing compression stockings
- Staying well hydrated

Always follow your healthcare provider’s advice regarding medication and travel.
